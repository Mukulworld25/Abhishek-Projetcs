Serving the Burpengary, Caboolture and Morayfield Communities.

Proactive Dental was established to serve the residents ofBurpengary, Caboolture, Morayfield, and surrounds. Since May 2010, we have served thousands of residents providing quality, gentle dental services at affordable prices. ranging from Implants, Veneers, Crowns, Bridges, White Fillings, Teeth whitening, Endodontic (root canal therapy), Extractions, Dentures, Mouthguards, Oral prophylaxis (cleaning) to Periodontic treatment (gum disease), Orthodontics(braces), Wisdom teeth extractions and Cerec(One day Crowns).

A Burpengary Dental Clinic where the focus is on YOU!

We find pride in our quality and pain-free dental services offered in a serene environment. Your dental health is our first priority. With a dedicated team of professionals, we have integrated modern technology with a warm, welcoming and compassionate approach. We are committed to listening to your dental needs and ensuring all treatment is absolutely pain-free. Should it be your first visit or a returning patient, we treat everyone as if they were our family. We are also one of very few dentists in the area offering interest-free, zero deposit payment plans allowing you to pay for treatment later, so you can get the care you need today.
