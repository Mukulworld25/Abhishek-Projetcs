Our Therapy Dog

Meet Winston,
a very good boy.

Fear and stress are the number one reasons people put off the dentist — so we did something about it. Winston is our trained therapy dog, here to help you feel calm, supported and in control from the moment you walk in.

Nervous about treatment? Just ask, and Winston will happily sit beside you during your appointment. Research shows a friendly dog can lower anxiety and blood pressure — and honestly, he's just lovely to have around.
