Dental Emergencies

At Proactive Dental, we understand the burden and stress associated with toothaches or other dental-related emergencies. Whether it’s a sore tooth, or an unfortunate accident that resulted in some broken teeth, the team at Proactive Dental are here to help.

We understand that sometimes it can take days or weeks to get in to see your regular dentist, and when you are in pain, waiting is the last thing you want to have to do. So, at Proactive Dental regardless of if you are a regular patient of ours or have never seen us before, we will endeavour to have emergency/toothache patients seen as soon as possible, usually same or following day. We are committed to ensuring that we do our best get you out of pain and ensure a good future outcome.

Proactive Dental is also open late for evening appointments during the week, and on Saturdays as we understand that it can often be difficult to organise appointments around existing schedules and work commitments. All patients are welcome at Proactive Dental, so if the need arises, please do not hesitate to contact our friendly team on 0754 331 569
