Our Dentists

Dr Aditya Athale

Dentist QLD Burpengary

After completing his Doctor of Dental Surgery from the University of Melbourne, Dr Adi has worked across Regional Victoria and Melbourne before finally making the move to sunny Queensland and the beautiful northern suburbs of Brisbane. Having worked in both Public and Private practice, he has gained valuable skills and established himself as a confident, sincere and ethical dentist. Dr Adi takes pride in providing the highest quality dentistry with a calm voice and a gentle manner.

“I like to treat all my patients as I would my immediate family members. I try to be as caring, compassionate and empathetic as I possibly can while helping my patients achieve their goals for their oral health.”

Dr Adi enjoys all aspects of general dentistry and attends regular education programs to expand his skills in order to provide his patients with the latest advancements in dentistry.

“Everyone has different priorities, expectations and goals for their oral health. I try to understand these and educate my patients about the various options available to them. Once they are aware of the advantages and limitations of their treatment decision, I try my best to help them achieve their desired outcomes and surpass their expectations.”

Outside of work Dr Adi loves to spend time with his wife and 2 year old daughter. He also regularly plays social sports like cricket, badminton and golf.

Dr. Diphuka

Dentist

Dr. Diphuka is a gentle and caring dentist who enjoys all aspects of general dentistry. She is committed to creating healthy and beautiful smiles and takes pride in helping patients feel comfortable and supported throughout their dental journey.

After graduating from the University of Queensland with a Bachelor of Dental Science (Honours), Dr. Diphuka has continued to grow her skills through attending regular professional development courses. She stays up to date with the latest dental technologies and techniques to provide patients with high-quality, evidence-based care.

Dr. Diphuka takes the time to listen to her patients and tailors treatment plans to suit their individual needs and goals. Her approachable and compassionate nature helps patients feel at ease, whether they are visiting for a routine check-up or more complex treatment.

Outside of dentistry, she enjoys taking walks, visiting local markets, and spending quality time with family and friends.
