Dental Bridges

A dental bridge is one of the many options that can help to replace single, or multiple missing teeth (, as seen in the picture?). A dental bridge is a fixed (non-removable) option that anchors to neighbouring teeth to replace the function and appearance of the missing tooth/teeth. It can be made using porcelain or ceramic/zirconia materials in shades that ensure a seamless blend with your existing teeth.

At Proactive Dental, suitable bridge cases can be completed in as little as two appointments, with a temporary bridge used in the interim to fill the gap. Our team also works closely with local dental laboratories and highly skilled technicians who can ensure that your bridge is of the highest quality and craftsmanship. To guarantee that the appearance of your bridge fits your expectations before cementation, a colour matching service with our lab technicians is also available.

Please don’t hesitate to get in contact to arrange a time to discuss the different replacement options available for missing teeth, such as dental bridges. Our friendly dentists will ensure that all the involved procedures and fees are explained thoroughly, so that you are armed with all the information needed to choose the treatment option that best suits you.
