General Dentistry

At Proactive Dental, our general dentistry services form the foundation of lifelong oral health. We believe in proactive, preventative care that helps you and your family avoid complex dental issues before they start. From routine check-ups and professional teeth cleanings to advanced restorative procedures, our experienced team provides gentle, high-quality care tailored to your unique needs.

Regular dental visits are essential not just for a bright smile, but for your overall well-being. During your comprehensive general check-up, our dentists will thoroughly examine your teeth, gums, and mouth, checking for early signs of decay, gum disease, and other oral health issues. We use state-of-the-art diagnostic tools, including digital X-rays and OPG scans, to get a complete picture of your oral health with minimal radiation exposure.

Our General Dentistry Services include:

  • Composite Dental Fillings: Tooth-coloured fillings that restore damaged teeth while maintaining a natural appearance.
  • Digital X-rays & OPG: Fast, precise imaging to diagnose hidden dental issues below the gumline.
  • Root Canal Treatment: A gentle, pain-relieving procedure to save infected or severely damaged teeth.
  • Dental Crowns: Custom-fit caps that strengthen and protect weakened, broken, or decayed teeth.
  • Dental Bridges: Durable, natural-looking restorations to replace one or more missing teeth.
  • Dental Implants: The gold standard for replacing missing teeth, offering a permanent, realistic solution.
  • Gum Health & Periodontal Care: Targeted treatments to prevent and manage gum disease, keeping your teeth secure.

We understand that visiting the dentist can cause anxiety for some patients. That is why Proactive Dental is designed to be a calm, welcoming environment. Our gentle dentists use advanced techniques to ensure your complete comfort at every stage of your treatment. We also offer flexible, interest-free payment plans so that you can receive the care you need without financial stress.
