Extractions

At Proactive Dental saving teeth is one of our main goals. In cases where extraction is the final option or if a patient wishes to have the tooth removed however, we will do so while ensuring the procedure is as straightforward as possible. Our dentists are all trained in the safe removal of teeth and always endeavour to make your time spent in the chair as short and pain-free as possible.

The team at Proactive Dental understand that having a tooth removed can be a very stress and anxiety-inducing procedure. Our aim is always to ensure that any discomfort felt during and after the extraction procedure is minimised. We use a numbing gel before giving local anaesthesia, and always have something enjoyable playing on our ceiling-mounted TVs for your entertainment during the procedure. We also offer nitrous oxide or ‘happy gas’ if requested, with our experienced and certified dentists.

Our team is always happy to answer any questions or organise consults with our approachable dentists, so feel free to contact us on0754 331 569today!
