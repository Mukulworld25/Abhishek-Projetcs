Clear Aligners

Clear Aligners

Invisible Braces

Do you want a straighter smile without the brackets and wires? With advancements in technology and specialized training in the field, our dentists can now straighten teeth without braces. We provide Invisalign and Clear Correct treatment which can gently nudge the teeth into the right place. Clear Aligners or Invisible braces can be used to straighten teeth without the need for any wires or braces. Treatment is quicker than with traditional braces, with most treatments taking just 6-18months.

Some benefits our patients enjoy the most about clear aligner braces:

o The braces are nearly invisible.
o Clear aligners are removable which makes it easier to chew and clean your teeth. Simply remove the aligners when eating and drinking.
o No changes needed to your diet because the aligners can be removed.
o Fewer appointments are needed for adjustments.
o Progress is easy to see and track.
o You get a 3D representation of your predicted final result.
o Free Bleaching before we start aligner treatment.

How can I get started?

We provide a no-obligation, free consultation to all new patients. During this visit, we will determine if you are a good candidate for clear aligners. We will explain each option clearly so you can make a decision which you feel most comfortable with. Intraoral Scans will be taken with our state of the art Cerec 3D scanner. Photographs and Xrays will also be required as part of the treatment planning process to give you the best possible results.

We will generate a 3D outcome simulator which will show you the final result, this will also be emailed to you to show friends and family. Once you are happy with the final result, the aligners generally take one week to be delivered from the United States. Most patients opt for the free whitening which is done in the first two weeks before starting aligners.
